Apparel Portfolio

Throughout this semester, I have been the Apparel Director for my sorority. I created merchandise, designs, and specialty clothing for Chi Omega. I took significant charge of this leading role as the director of apparel through monthly clothing drops, meetings, and specific products for events. I excelled in my designs by becoming proficient in Canva, Photoshop, and Adobe Illustrator. While collaborating with manufacturers, I coordinate each specific detail within the design created and watch it come to life with each order having over 200 purchases.
